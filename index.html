WELCOME TO SGIT

SriGuru Institute of Technology (SGIT) is an efficacious student-centered Institution which has been established in the year 2009 by SriGuru Educational Trust, approved by All India Council for technical Education, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. Our Institution is ahead in its commitment to learning, research,innovation and entrepreneurship, so that our graduates are prepared to succeed globally.An enlightened management under the visionary guidance of Thiru.S.Subramanian as President and Thiru.R.Natarajan (Sakthi Coach) as Correspondent.
