Placement

Placement Overview

The Corporate Relations team at SriGuru Institute of Technology provides its outstanding access to its students by taking every opportunity to look for matches with corporate needs and look forward to connecting even more closely. Being enthused with a smooth relationship with corporations of all sizes, from startups to mature, successful enterprises, placement team provides all the resources to their students to make them successful in a rapidly changing business environment. Running with a team of dynamic individuals who symbolize the spirit in their professionalism, creativity and passion, our corporate Head and coordinators make the best opportunities enabling every student to realize his/her dream.

Training the young minds make them aspire to excel in their interviews with high confidence and determination. The Training Cell offers intensive career oriented training to its students which results in harvesting Industry ready and Employable professionals.
