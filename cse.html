The programme is innovatively designed to offer a balanced coverage of hardware and software with emphasis on various aspects of software including programming.The Department helps the students to get familiarized with different protocols and network components through state-of- art-laboratories equipped with rich repository of latest software.The Department organizes enrichment programmes like Workshops, Seminars and Conferences to provide opportunities for the students to interact with eminent personalities in the field of software.
