Eligibility Criteria for M.E.Courses

Candidates who have obtained B.E through Lateral Entry or Part Time B.E after Diploma through Lateral Entry (after qualifying 10+2) or after Part Time Diploma are also considered eligible.
Candidates admitted through Lateral entry in Degree courses are not eligible except B.E / B.Tech degree courses.
Candidates with B.E / B.Tech / MCA / M.Sc degrees obtained through Week end courses and B.E / B.Tech through Distance mode are not eligible.
Candidates who have not studied 10th, Plus Two / Diploma as prescribed are not eligible.
The qualifying UG Degree awarded by Universities should conform to the minimum standards of instruction for grant of the First Degree through Formal / Non Formal / Distance (wherever applicable ) Education prescribed by UGC/AICTE.

A pass in HSC (Academic with Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry) or its equivalent with a minimum average percentage in the relevant subjects as prescribed below.

For Communities other than BC / BC (Muslims) / MBC & DNC / SC /ST & Other Communities – A minimum average of 50% and above in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry put together.
For BC / BCM Communities – A minimum average of 45% and above in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry put together.
For MBC & DNC Communities – A minimum average of 40% and above in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry put together.
For SC / ST – belonging to Tamilnadu, a mere pass (40%) in the qualifying examination with Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.

A pass in any one of the HSC (Vocational Stream) with Engineering/Technology Vocational subjects and any ONE or TWO of the related subjects namely Mathematics, Physics or Chemistry with a minimum average percentage in the relevant subjects as listed below.


For Communities other than BC / BC (Muslims) / MBC & DNC / SC /ST & Other Communities – A minimum average of 50% and above in Vocational Theory subject and Practicals put together.
For BC / BCM Communities- A minimum average of 45% and above in Vocational Theory subject and Practicals put together.
For MBC & DNC Communities – A minimum average of 40% and above in Vocational Theory subject and Practicals put together.
For SC / ST – belonging to Tamilnadu a mere pass (40%) in the qualifying examination with Vocational subjects prescribed and Mathematics or Physics or Chemistry. Vocational Subjects prescribed for B.E/B.Tech Degree Admission
